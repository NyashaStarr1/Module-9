Featured Case

Jaliek’s Note

Type: Missing • Status: Ongoing • Location: New York

Jaliek Rainwalker was just 12 when he disappeared from Greenwich, New York in 2007. Labeled a “troubled” child and shuffled between adoptive homes, he left behind a cryptic note the night he went missing. This episode explores the unanswered questions, shifting stories, and unusual behaviors that followed.

🎤 Your Host

Nya Starr

Creator & Host — The Noir Cases

Nya Starr is the creator and host of The Noir Cases, where she examines true crime through a systemic and research-driven lens. Holding a master’s degree in Criminal Justice and grounded in her background in sociology, she brings academic rigor and lived experience to every investigation.

An Air Force veteran and longtime advocate, her activism began in her college years, where she organized and led efforts confronting racism and institutional inequity. Today, her work challenges narratives, amplifies marginalized voices, and questions the systems that shape justice.
